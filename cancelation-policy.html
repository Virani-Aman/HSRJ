Cancellation Policy

In case of 'No Show' for reasons not mentioned, the payment will not be refunded or adjusted. Rescheduling can only be done 24 hours prior to your actual scheduled session. or rescheduling the same process will be followed as to booking a new session. Kindly choose from options offered. If an appointment is rescheduled twice and still there is a no show you will be charged for the full session.
